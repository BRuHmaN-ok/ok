Some cool creatures in Ark

There are Way to many Dinosaurs in this game, so I can't list every single one. There are three types of creatures in this game flying creatures, marine creatures, and land creatures. Not all of the creatures in ark are dinos, There are stuff like bugs, Arthropod, mammals and many more. There are also bosses in the game which reward you when you kill them. All these creatures spawn in various locations, there are a lot of DLC's for this game which introduce a new map and new creatures.
