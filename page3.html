Step by Step

I will show you somethings that can help you get started.

The first thing that you need to do when you spawn in is to punch some trees and collect some stone from the ground. Equip a empty hand to gather materials from bushes and collect fiber and berrys. you can go to your inventory by pressing "i", and you can also go to the crafting menu to make some basic tools. Make a stone picaxe, axe and a wooden club to gather materials and protect yourself for a little while. Try to gather wood and thach to make a small house and a bed to set your spawn. Try to collect some metal to make some more advanced weapons. You should be able to identify the diffrence between stone and metal, metal has a shade of yellow and reflects light. Try to kill small animals to get some hide and meat. You can cook the meat or wait for it to spoil so you can make narcotics.

You can find lots of tips and tricks on youtube that help you a lot. Try to get a triceratops and make a saddle for it. When your riding around you can cut down trees and collect lots of matrials. You can almost tame anything in this game, but check before you approach it.
